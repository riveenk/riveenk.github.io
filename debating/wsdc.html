Introduction to World Schools Debating Championship Format

Speech Length

08:00 (+ 15s Grace)

Reply Length

04:00 (+ 15s Grace)

Team Composition

Proposition

This team supports the motion

Opposition

This team opposes the motion

Speaker Positions with Order

1: Proposition First Speaker

  • Introduce Definitions
  • Introduce Context
  • Introduce a Module (if needed)
  • Introduce Main Argument (ideally two)

2: Opposition First Speaker

  • Introduce/ Challenge Definitions
  • Introduce Context
  • Introduce a Module (if needed)
  • Introduce Main Argument (ideally two)

3: Proposition Second Speaker

  • Supplement First Speaker's Case
  • Introduce New Argument(s)
  • Rebut the Opposition Case

4: Opposition Second Speaker

  • Supplement First Speaker's Case
  • Introduce New Argument(s)
  • Rebut the Proposition Case

5: Proposition Third Speaker

  • Introduce a Clash Point Analysis
  • Discuss Harms vs. Benefits
  • Rebut the Opposition Case (if needed)

6: Opposition Third Speaker

  • Introduce a Clash Point Analysis
  • Discuss Harms vs. Benefits
  • Rebut the Proposition Case (if needed)

7: Opposition Reply Speaker

  • Summarise Your Case
  • Summarise the Opposition case
  • Present Why Your Case is Better

8: Proposition Reply Speaker

  • Summarise Your Case
  • Summarise the Proposition case
  • Present Why Your Case is Better

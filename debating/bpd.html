Speaker Positions with Order

1: Prime Minister

  • Introduce Definitions
  • Introduce Context
  • Introduce a Module (if needed)
  • Introduce Main Argument (ideally two)

2: Opposition Leader

  • Introduce/ Challenge Definitions
  • Introduce Context
  • Introduce a Module (if needed)
  • Introduce Main Argument (ideally two)

3: Deputy Prime Minister

  • Supplement First Speaker's Case
  • Introduce New Argument(s)
  • Rebut the Opposition Case

4: Deputy Opposition Leader

  • Supplement First Speaker's Case
  • Introduce New Argument(s)
  • Rebut the Proposition Case

3: Member of Government

  • Introduce New Argument(s)
  • Build on the Opening Bench
  • Rebut the Opposition Case

4: Member of Opposition

  • Introduce New Argument(s)
  • Build on the Opening Bench
  • Rebut the Proposition Case

5: Government Whip Speaker

  • Introduce a Clash Point Analysis
  • Imply Why You're Better than Opening
  • Rebut the Opposition Case (if needed)

6: Opposition Whip Speaker

  • Introduce a Clash Point Analysis
  • Imply Why You're Better than Opening
  • Rebut the Proposition Case (if needed)

Maybe in a Few Years

(Poem): Friendship | 09 April, 2025


Maybe in a few years
I will see you holding a child
or twins, I'll be an uncle
holding those balls of sunshine

Maybe in a few years
I will see you teary eyed
Next to me, as she walks down
the aisle for you

Maybe in a few years
I will hear my phone ring and
I will see all of your faces
again, and we will laugh like before

Maybe in a few years
I will be telling people about you
Our late night calls, our
fights and jokes

But until those maybe years
happen, I will be on call with you
Laughing till I can't breathe
Hours after sun has set

But until those maybe years
happen, I will take screenshots of
us, and make plans that may
happen, and end every call with love

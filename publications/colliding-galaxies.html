Colliding Galaxies

Poem: Romance | Sheet 8


Collided galaxies, you and I
My scattered stars
Some of yours in place of mine
They burn my heart,
They burn a faint eerie glow
Take them please, take them back

Before you become a part of me
The glow still haunts me
The light I flew at,
A moth to your flame

Now my wounds bleed of you
My scars speak a tale of you
Your stars keep them alive
Take them back, hush the tales
Pretend we never met
Just two galaxies,
Passing each other, in orbit
A random bridge between us
A starry path to my heart
Just missing yours

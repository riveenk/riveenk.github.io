Maybe the Gods are playing Cricket

Poem: Crazy | 08 May, 2025


The sky is dark and the wind is loud
Rain clouds have gathered around
Somewhere near, a thundering sound
I wait and see before I look round

It must be a six, that thunderous shot
Across the boundary the ball was shot
That lightning must be all the camera shots
Flashing to capture that wonderful shot

Maybe the gods are playing cricket
The wind howls through the thriving thicket
The ball flew over the fence, picket
or just brushed past the anxious wicket

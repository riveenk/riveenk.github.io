Coffee Shop

Poem - Love | 30 May, 2023

You are sitting by the window,
Book in hand, your coffee clentched-
Eyes roaming around worlds of high fantasy
And magic and crime and mystery and thrill.

From across the room I'm reading you,
Happy. Sad. Amazed. Afraid. The feelings
flowing fluid inside me interchanging
With and across one another: Tangled.

I'm staring and it's obvious: Eyes-Book-Coffee-Eyes;
People notice: Book-Eyes-Coffee-Eyes;
You notice: Book-Eyes-Coffee-Eyes-Eyes;
You smile: Eyes-Smile-Smile-Eyes.

You stand up, and walk... towards me (And
I'm screaming inside) - then past me...
(What??? [that is not supposed to happen] Why???
[Maybe she missed? (forgot? -how?)] Why???)
And from behind, a hand- it's you.
Your warm and soft and welcoming hand.
I take it - with you, and walk out the cafe;
To write our own story. Together.
